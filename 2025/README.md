# 2025 Season summary

The 2025 Formula 1 season was marked by intense battles between the top drivers, with several championship contenders showcasing consistent performance across the calendar. Oscar Piastri and Lando Norris, driving for McLaren, and Max Verstappen of Red Bull Racing dominated the headlines, each securing multiple victories that shaped the championship standings. The season saw thrilling races across traditional circuits like Monaco and Silverstone, as well as newer venues such as Las Vegas, keeping fans on the edge of their seats until the final round in Abu Dhabi.

### Victory Tally
* **Lando Norris**: 7 Wins
*  **Oscar Piastri**: 7 Wins
* **Max Verstappen**: 8 Wins
*  **George Russell**: 2 Wins


The 2025 racing season has featured an incredibly close fight among the grid's top performers. **Max Verstappen** and **Oscar Piastri** namely secured 8 and 7 victories, yet despite those win counts, the ultimate 2025 champion based on total points was Lando Norris.

**Lando Norris** achieved 7 wins while consistently scoring the high points necessary to battle Piastri and Verstappen. Completing the lead group is **George Russell**, who successfully fought his way to 2 victories over the course of the season.

## Teams and drivers

The 2025 grid features stability at Ferrari, McLaren, and Mercedes, alongside high-profile moves like Lewis Hamilton joining Ferrari and Carlos Sainz moving to Williams with respect to 2024 season, and Andrea Kimi Antonelli debuting at Mercedes to replace Hamilton.

Mid-season drama centered on the second seats at Red Bull and Alpine, with Yuki Tsunoda replacing Liam Lawson and Franco Colapinto stepping in for Jack Doohan.
